# Low-Fidelity Wireframe: UX Flow for Requesting to Join a Team

---

## 1. Dashboard or Teams List

+-----------------------------------------------+
| [Teams List]                                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| [Team 1]   [Request to Join] (on hover)       |
| [Team 2]   [Request to Join] (on hover)       |
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+

---

## 2. Click "Request to Join Team" → Open Modal or Navigate to Form

+-------------------------------+
|      Request to Join a Team   |
+-------------------------------+
| [Enter Team Name or Code]     |
| [Optional: Message to Admin]  |
| [Request to Join Button] [Cancel] |
+-------------------------------+

---

## 3. Validation/Error State

+-------------------------------+
| [Error: Invalid team code or name] |
+-------------------------------+

---

## 4. Pending State

+-------------------------------+
| [Request sent. Waiting for approval.] |
+-------------------------------+

---

## 5. Success State (After Approval)

+-------------------------------+
| [Success: You have joined the team!] |
+-------------------------------+

---

## 6. Return to Teams List

+-----------------------------------------------+
| [Teams List updated with joined team]         |
+-----------------------------------------------+

---

This flow uses a request-to-join process, optionally allowing a message to the admin, and includes pending/approval states for better team management context.
